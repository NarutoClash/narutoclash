# **App Name**: Shinobi Arena

## Core Features:

- News Menu: Access the latest game news and announcements.
- Status Menu: View your character's current status and statistics.
- Elements Menu: Manage and customize your character's elemental affinities.
- Weapons Menu: Equip and manage your character's weapons.
- Missions Menu: Accept and track available missions.
- Character Selection: Select from a diverse roster of characters, each with unique abilities.
- Arena Battles: Engage in real-time battles with other players in various iconic Naruto-themed arenas.
- Jutsu Customization: Customize your character's jutsu (ninja techniques) to create unique combat strategies.
- Ranked Mode: Compete in ranked matches to climb the leaderboards and prove your skills.
- AI Opponents: Fight against AI-controlled opponents for practice and offline gameplay.
- Team Battles: Form teams with friends and participate in team-based battles.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep orange (#FF851B), reminiscent of Naruto's determination.
- Background color: Light tan (#F5E7D0), inspired by the color of parchment scrolls.
- Accent color: Electric blue (#7DF9FF) suggesting chakra and energy.
- Body and headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a sans-serif for a modern, computerized feel.
- Icons will incorporate stylized shuriken and kunai to represent actions, while character abilities are represented by simplified, color-coded symbols of their nature transformations (fire, water, earth, etc).
- The main menu should use a dynamic layout that responds to different screen sizes, ensuring compatibility across devices; interactive tutorials overlayed on the main screens will guide new players; the style and format of NarutoPlayer 2.0 and 3.0 serve as direct inspiration for this title's organization of information, as well as navigation between pages.
- Subtle animations will accompany character jutsu activation and impact, enhancing the impact and satisfaction of attacks.
